Pokemon Generation and Interpolation

This is an app for trying out a VAE trained on 128x128 Pokemon images. See https://www.benedekpapp.page/projects/pokemon-generation-and-interpolation. Pick what you want to do from the tabs below. This app includes a subset of the images used in training.

Interpolation

Interpolation works by selecting 2 existing images, encoding them to the latent space. A new image is generated from an interpolation between these two vectors. Pick two images from below to be: Image A and Image B
